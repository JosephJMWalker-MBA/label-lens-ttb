## Matrix conclusion

The product has demonstrated components, but maturity is constrained by the missing human decision loop and unknown usefulness. “Durable submission” is not labeled DEMONSTRATED because one deployed waiting submission observed during the audit could not be opened; this conservative label does not generalize the dated observation or deny the API tests.
