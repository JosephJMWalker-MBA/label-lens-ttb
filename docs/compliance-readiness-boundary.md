# Compliance Readiness Boundary

## Purpose

This prototype is intentionally standalone. It is designed to demonstrate architecture, governance, OCR, verification, accessibility, and operational workflow—not direct integration with COLA or a production federal environment.

## Principles

- Compliance-ready, not compliance-certified.
- Local-first architecture; cloud AI optional.
- Server-side secrets only.
- Temporary processing by default.
- Explainable, deterministic rule engine.
- Human review remains authoritative.
- Versioned rules, models, datasets, and audit decisions.

## Explicit Non-Goals

- No COLA integration.
- No production
