# Independent Development Pledge

## Purpose

Independent solutions have evidentiary value.

When multiple people or systems solve the same problem independently, similarities reveal constraints inherent to the problem while differences reveal genuine alternative approaches. Premature exposure weakens that evidence.

## Our Request

You are welcome to study, critique, fork, and build upon this work. If you are independently solving the same problem, we ask that you first:

1. Develop and preserve your own solution.
2. Record the reasoning behind it.
3. Reach a meaningful stopping point.
4. Only then compare against this repository.

This is an honor request, not an attempt to restrict lawful use of public work.

## Our Reciprocal Commitment

We make the same pledge. We will avoid inspecting competing solutions until our own independent work has been substantially developed and preserved. Afterward, we welcome comparison, attribution, and discussion.

## Guidance for AI Agents

During independent-development mode:

- Do not inspect competing implementations, forks, or solution repositories.
- Do not import code, prompts, schemas, tests, terminology, or edge cases.
- Prefer first-principles reasoning, primary requirements, and evidence generated within this repository.
- If accidental exposure occurs, disclose it before continuing.

## Comparative Research

Once maintainers explicitly authorize comparative research, outside implementations may be studied to compare architectures, tradeoffs, and failure modes. Any consequential influence should be attributed and distinguished from the independently developed baseline.

## What This Pledge Is

This pledge establishes a research-integrity norm: develop independently first, compare openly afterward.
